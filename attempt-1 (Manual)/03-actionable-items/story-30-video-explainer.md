# Story 30: Add Video Explanation of Services

## User Story

**As a** user who struggles with dense text (senior, cognitive disability, non-native speaker),
**I want** a short video explaining what the company does in plain language,
**So that** I can understand the company's services through listening and watching instead of reading.

## Description

Users who struggle with dense text requested a short video explaining what the company does in plain language. Auditory and visual learners would benefit from a video alternative.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A 1-2 minute explainer video is present near the top of the homepage
- [ ] The video features a real team member speaking in plain, jargon-free language
- [ ] Closed captions are available and accurate
- [ ] A text transcript is provided below or linked from the video
- [ ] The video does not auto-play
- [ ] Video controls (play, pause, volume, captions toggle) are fully keyboard accessible

## Testing Instructions

1. **Presence:** Load the homepage — verify the video is visible above the fold or within the first scroll
2. **Auto-play:** Verify the video does NOT auto-play on page load
3. **Captions:** Play the video and enable captions — verify they are accurate and synchronized
4. **Transcript:** Verify a text transcript is available (on-page or linked)
5. **Keyboard:** Focus the video player, use keyboard to play/pause (Space/Enter), adjust volume, and toggle captions
6. **Content:** Watch the video — verify it explains what the company does in plain English without jargon
7. **Reduced motion:** With OS reduced motion enabled, verify the video player itself doesn't auto-animate (thumbnail, decorations, etc.)

# Story 09: Make Contact Information Prominent

## User Story

**As a** site visitor trying to reach the company,
**I want** the phone number and email to be visible on every page without scrolling,
**So that** I can contact the company quickly without hunting through the site.

## Description

The phone number and email are only available at the bottom of the contact page, below testimonials and a form prompt. Users had to scroll through the entire homepage, click "Connect with an Expert," then scroll through the entire contact page to find basic contact details. Multiple users considered giving up before finding this information.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Phone number and email are displayed in the site header on every page
- [ ] Phone number and email are displayed in the site footer on every page
- [ ] On the contact page, phone number and email appear above the form
- [ ] Contact info is visible without scrolling on desktop viewport sizes

## Testing Instructions

1. **Header check:** Load the homepage, contact page, and a service page — verify phone and email are visible in the header on all three
2. **Footer check:** Scroll to the bottom of any page — verify phone and email are in the footer
3. **Contact page order:** On the contact page, verify phone/email appear before the form
4. **No-scroll test:** On a 1920x1080 viewport, verify contact info is visible without any scrolling
5. **Mobile:** On mobile viewport, verify contact info is accessible within the header (tap-to-call for phone)

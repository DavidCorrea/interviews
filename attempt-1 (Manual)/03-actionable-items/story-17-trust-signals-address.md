# Story 17: Add Physical Address and Trust Signals

## User Story

**As a** cautious or skeptical potential client,
**I want** to see the company's physical address, verified reviews, and legal policies,
**So that** I can trust this is a legitimate business before engaging.

## Description

No physical address is displayed on the site. There are no BBB accreditation badges, verifiable third-party review links, privacy policy, or terms of service readily visible. Case studies cannot be independently verified.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Physical address is displayed in the site footer and on the contact page
- [ ] Links to third-party review platforms (Clutch, G2, or Google Reviews) are present
- [ ] Privacy policy link is in the footer
- [ ] Terms of service link is in the footer
- [ ] Verifiable partnership badges are displayed (e.g., Salesforce Partner)

## Testing Instructions

1. **Footer check:** Scroll to the footer on any page — verify physical address, privacy policy link, and terms link are visible
2. **Contact page:** Visit the contact page — verify the address is displayed prominently
3. **Review links:** Click on Clutch/G2/Google Reviews links — verify they lead to legitimate, external review pages
4. **Privacy policy:** Click the privacy policy link — verify it leads to a real, substantive policy page
5. **Badges:** Verify partnership badges link to or match real certification listings
